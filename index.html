Joe Varberg

I am an interdisciplinary cell biologist using the powers of yeast genetics and advanced microscopy techniques to study the structure and organization of the nucleus. Currently, I live in the Kansas City metro where I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Stowers Institute for Medical Research. I’m passionate about data visualization, scientific communication and building the scientific research community in the Kansas City region. When I’m not imaging on the microscope or wrangling data in R, I enjoy running/biking on the trails in Shawnee, golfing, and hunting for the freshest coffee beans in KC.
